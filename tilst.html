New Mission in
Tilst

After a long search, we have finally found new premises, so we can get started again with what we love most: to build cool challenges for you! This time we have allied ourselves with a group of skilled theater technicians, to ensure that the quality of both game design, puzzles, decorations and effects will be among the best in Denmark.

The address for our new department will be Langkærvej 2B, 8381 Tilst - close to Bilka. In the long run, the department will accommodate 5 different escape room experiences, and we have selected themes carefully so that there will be something for everyone. See i.a. forward to colorful adventures for the whole family, competitive duels between large groups, hardcore challenges for the sharpest brains, and disturbing thrills for those who have ice in their stomachs.

Follow the latest updates about Escapist Tilst on our Facebook page!
