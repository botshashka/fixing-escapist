The Great Diamond Heist

The Aarhus National Museum recently acquired the incredibly rare blue Bol’Shoy diamond from a Russian museum, and you and your team of experienced art thieves are now itching to get your hands on it! Can you make your way through the crafty old curator’s office, decipher his tricky codes and riddles, and escape with the valuable gem before the police arrives?
