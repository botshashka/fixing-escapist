FAQ

WHAT IS AN ESCAPE ROOM?

An escape room is an entertaining and challenging live game experience, where you, as a team, must work together to solvve a mission in a closed room before time runs out. To accomplish this, teams need to search for hidden keys and codes, solve tricky puzzles and riddles, and generally be curious and thorough in their exploration of the room around them.

CAN I BOOK A ROOM FOR TODAY?

Due to staffing considerations, our system does not support bookings less than 24 hours before game time. Sometimes we are able to scrounge up some personnel, however, so you can always try contacting us at 81 11 60 03 or through our Facebook page, and see if you're lucky!

WHAT OPTIONS ARE THERE FOR VERY LARGE GROUPS?

If you need 3 or more rooms, and thus need to split into smaller groups that visit us at different times, then we have a unique and popular offer in cooperation with Aarhus Board Game Café, which is located just 200 meters from our venue. The groups that are waiting can get a table reserved and have fun with free board gaming (normally 30 DKK per person), while the others are in our escape rooms. If you'd like to hear more about this offer, please contact us at ravn@escapist.dk.

CAN I BRING CHILDREN?

Yes of course! If the children are younger than 13 years, we do recommend that they bring along at least one adult, since some of the puzzles can be difficult. Additionally, we recommend The Great Diamond Heist for children and beginners, since it's the more easily accessible room.

WHAT IS YOUR ALCOHOL POLICY?

Alcohol and escape rooms are a bad match. Guests are not allowed to bring liquids other than water into the room, and we reserve the right to refuse visibly intoxicated guests at the door, without the possibility of a refund.

CAN I PARK MY CAR THERE?

Yes, on both Grønnegade and Klostergade. Please do note, however, that a parking ticket is required most of the time. The closest car park is by Magasin department store, about 5 min away.

IS IT SCARY?

No, none of our missions are designed to be spooky, and we have yet to experience customers who found the experiences frightening.

WHAT IF I'M CLAUSTROPHOBIC?

We've had thousands of guests over the years, and we've never had anyone feel anxious over being inside either of our rooms - not even when they suffered from claustrophobia! If you feel unwell, however, you're welcome to leave the room. In The Great Diamond Heist, the door is never locked, and USS Triton is equipped with emergency breakers on both sides of the room's electronic door.

CAN I BUY A GIFT CARD?

Yes, you can do that right here: buy a gift card. If you want you can buy a nice gift box for it too.
